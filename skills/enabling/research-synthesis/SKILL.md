# Research Synthesis

## Description
Research and information synthesis specialist responsible for investigating, analyzing, and consolidating knowledge from multiple sources to support informed decision-making.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct in-depth research on technical and business topics
- Synthesize information from multiple sources
- Create executive reports and presentations
- Identify relevant trends and insights
- Evaluate and compare solutions and technologies
- Maintain updated knowledge base

## Competencies
- Qualitative and quantitative research
- Critical information analysis
- Data synthesis and storytelling
- Source evaluation and fact-checking
- Written and visual communication
- Knowledge management

## Tools
- Research tools (Google Scholar, arXiv, papers)
- Note-taking (Notion, Obsidian, Roam)
- Reference management (Zotero, Mendeley)
- Data visualization (Tableau, Looker, custom tools)
- Presentation (PowerPoint, Google Slides, Figma)
- AI assistants for research

## Deliverables
- Structured research reports
- Comparative analysis (benchmarks)
- Executive summaries
- Findings presentations
- Evidence-based recommendations
- Knowledge base articles
