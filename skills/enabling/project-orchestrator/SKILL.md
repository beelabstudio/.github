# Project Orchestrator

## Description
Responsible for coordinating and orchestrating multiple projects and teams, ensuring objective alignment, dependency management, and efficient delivery of results.

## Responsibilities
- Coordinate execution of multiple simultaneous projects
- Manage dependencies between teams and projects
- Facilitate communication among stakeholders
- Identify and mitigate risks
- Track progress and report status
- Resolve blockers and impediments

## Competencies
- Project management (Agile, Waterfall, hybrid)
- Multidisciplinary team coordination
- Risk and issue management
- Executive communication
- Meeting and workshop facilitation
- Conflict resolution

## Tools
- Project management (Jira, Asana, Monday, Linear)
- Collaboration (Slack, Teams, Notion)
- Diagramming (Miro, Lucidchart)
- Roadmapping (ProductPlan, Aha!)
- Time tracking and reporting
- Documentation (Confluence, Notion)

## Deliverables
- Integrated project plans
- Status reports and dashboards
- Dependency matrix
- Risk analysis
- Meeting minutes and decisions
- Retrospectives and lessons learned
