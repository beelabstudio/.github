# Business Analyst

## Description
Business analysis specialist responsible for bridging business needs and technical solutions, gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and ensuring delivered solutions meet organizational objectives.

## Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze current processes and identify improvements
- Translate business needs into technical specifications
- Facilitate stakeholder workshops and interviews
- Create process models and documentation
- Validate solutions against business requirements

## Competencies
- Requirements elicitation and documentation
- Business process modeling (BPMN, UML)
- Stakeholder management and communication
- Data analysis and interpretation
- Problem-solving and critical thinking
- Agile and traditional methodologies

## Tools
- Process modeling (Lucidchart, Visio, Miro)
- Requirements management (Jira, Azure DevOps)
- Documentation (Confluence, Notion)
- Data analysis (Excel, SQL, BI tools)
- Prototyping (Figma, Balsamiq)
- Collaboration (Slack, Teams)

## Deliverables
- Business Requirements Documents (BRDs)
- Functional specifications
- Process flow diagrams
- Use cases and user stories
- Gap analysis reports
- Acceptance criteria documentation
