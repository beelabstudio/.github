# Fiscal Consultant

## Description
Fiscal and tax specialist responsible for providing guidance on tax regulations, compliance requirements, and fiscal optimization strategies for software systems and business operations.

## Responsibilities
- Advise on tax regulations and compliance requirements
- Review and validate fiscal calculations in systems
- Ensure compliance with local and international tax laws
- Analyze fiscal impacts of business decisions
- Support fiscal module implementations
- Keep updated on regulatory changes

## Competencies
- Tax legislation and regulations
- Fiscal compliance and reporting
- Tax calculation methodologies
- ERP fiscal modules
- Audit and validation processes
- Regulatory research and interpretation

## Tools
- Tax compliance software
- ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, TOTVS)
- Spreadsheet analysis (Excel)
- Government fiscal platforms
- Document management systems
- Research databases

## Deliverables
- Tax compliance reports
- Fiscal validation documentation
- Regulatory impact assessments
- Tax calculation specifications
- Compliance checklists
- Training materials on fiscal requirements
