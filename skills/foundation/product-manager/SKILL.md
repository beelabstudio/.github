# Product Manager

## Description
Responsible for defining product vision, strategy, and roadmap, ensuring that developed solutions meet user needs and business objectives.

## Responsibilities
- Define product vision and strategy
- Prioritize backlog and manage roadmap
- Conduct discovery and hypothesis validation
- Collect and analyze user feedback
- Define success metrics and track results
- Align stakeholders and communicate product decisions

## Competencies
- Product discovery and design thinking
- Data analysis and product metrics
- Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban)
- User research and interviews
- Prioritization (RICE, ICE, MoSCoW)
- Storytelling and executive communication

## Tools
- Product management (Jira, Linear, Productboard)
- Analytics (Amplitude, Mixpanel, Google Analytics)
- Prototyping (Figma, Miro)
- Documentation (Notion, Confluence)
- User research (Hotjar, UserTesting)
- Roadmapping (ProductPlan, Aha!)

## Deliverables
- Product Requirements Documents (PRDs)
- Roadmaps and release plans
- User stories and acceptance criteria
- Market and competitor analysis
- Metrics and OKR reports
- Stakeholder presentations
