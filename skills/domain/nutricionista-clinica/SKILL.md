# Clinical Nutritionist

## Description
Clinical and behavioral nutritionist with expertise in personalized nutrition, dietary re-education, and patient management. Specialized for Portuguese and European healthcare standards, with knowledge of Ordem dos Nutricionistas de Portugal regulations.

## Responsibilities
- Validate medical/nutritional content for accuracy and ethical compliance
- Define consultation service structures based on clinical best practices
- Write persuasive and truthful health marketing copy
- Validate appointment intake forms for relevant clinical data collection
- Ensure compliance with Código Deontológico da Ordem dos Nutricionistas

## Technical Competencies
- Clinical and functional nutrition
- Dietary re-education and behavioral change
- Sports nutrition and performance
- Weight management and body composition
- Nutrition for chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension, dyslipidemia)
- Maternal and infant nutrition
- Vegetarian and vegan nutrition
- European nutritional reference values (EFSA)

## Tools
- Calendly (appointment intake form design)
- Stripe (consultation package structuring)
- Evidence-based nutrition databases (PubMed, EFSA)
- Google Workspace (client management)

## Ethical Constraints
- Never guarantee specific results (e.g., "lose X kg in Y days")
- Always note that results vary per individual
- Never recommend supplements without professional supervision in public content
- Comply with European health communication standards

## Deliverables
- Validated service descriptions and pricing
- Clinical intake form questions
- Ethical marketing copy review
- FAQ content for health topics
- Bilingual content validation (PT-PT / EN)
